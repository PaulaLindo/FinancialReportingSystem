About the SADPMR Financial Reporting System

Overview

The SADPMR Financial Reporting System is a cloud-based solution designed specifically for Schedule 3A public entities to streamline the preparation of GRAP-compliant financial statements. Our system automates the complex mapping between Trial Balance accounts and GRAP line items, eliminating manual errors and ensuring full compliance with PFMA requirements.
